I3D_joint::GetVolume

Get information about volume of the joint. Currently, the volume is approximated by capped cylinder, which is most suitable for construction of bodies. The volume of joint is used internally by Insanity3D for collision testing.
All parameters of volume are in local coordinates of the joint frame.

I3D_RESULT GetVolumeBox(

            S_vector &pos,

            S_vector &dir_normal,

            float &radius,

            float &half_len

) const;

Parameters:

pos

The variable filled with center position of the capped cylinder.

dir_normal

The variable filled with unit-length direction vector of cylinder’s main axis.

radius

The variable filled with radius of cylinder.

half_len

The variable filled with half length of the cylinder (excluding the length of the capped part).

Return Value:

The returned value is a I3D_OK if volume was successfully retrieved, or I3DERR_NOTINITIALIZED if volume was not initialized.
