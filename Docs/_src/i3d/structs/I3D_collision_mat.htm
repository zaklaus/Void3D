I3D_collision_mat

This structure keeps information about collision material (which are assigned to I3D_volume and I3D_visual frames), and is used during loading for mapping of material name to index.
The information is also used for specification how the collision materials are to be visualized

The structure is declared as follows:

struct I3D_collision_mat{
   C_str name;                //name of material
   dword color;               //visualization color
};

Members:

name

Name of material.

color

Visualization color.
