I3D Lock Flags

Lock flags are used when object is locked (vertices, faces, etc) to specify behavior taken during or after lock.
 Following flags are defined:

Flag

Description

I3DLOCK_XYZ

Position of vertex will modified during lock.

I3DLOCK_NORMAL

Normal of vertex will be modified during lock.

I3DLOCK_UV

UV coordinates of vertex will be modified during lock.

I3DLOCK_DIFFUSE

Diffuse color of vertex will be modified during lock.

I3DLOCK_SPECULAR

Specular color of vertex will be modified during lock.

I3DLOCK_ALPHA

Vertex alpha will be modified during lock.

I3DLOCK_VERTEX

Combination of all vertex-modifier flags.

I3DLOCK_INDEX

Face index will be modified during lock.

I3DLOCK_NO_LIGHT_RESET

Flag specifying that cached light information in vertices may remain valid.
